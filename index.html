About JGR College

JGR College offers a rigorous and authentic Islamic education rooted in the traditional Dars-e-Nizami syllabus. Our mission is to preserve sacred knowledge while training students to serve communities with wisdom, sincerity, and excellence.

Classical Books We Teach

Fiqh

Al-Hidāyah, Kanz al-Daqā’iq, Usūl al-Fiqh texts

Hadith

Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Mishkat al-Masabih

Tafsir

Tafsir al-Jalalayn, classical Qur’anic commentaries

Arabic Sciences

Al-Kāfiyah, Sharh Jami, Sarf & Nahw mastery

Our Faculty & Expertise

Our lead teacher has over 25 years of teaching experience and has studied under renowned scholars of the Ahl al-Sunnah tradition. Our faculty combines deep scholarship with spiritual mentorship, ensuring students receive authentic and well-rounded Islamic education.


JGR College has also published scholarly works through Jamaat Raza-e-Mustafa, contributing to the preservation and dissemination of Sunni Islamic knowledge.

Our Impact

By the grace of Allah, our graduates are now teaching in mosques and institutions across the North West, leading prayers, delivering lessons, and serving the Ummah with sound knowledge and exemplary character.
