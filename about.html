Our Headteacher

With over 25 years of teaching experience, our Headteacher is a graduate of the prestigious Al Jamia Ashrafiyah Mubarakpur. He is a recognized master in Fiqh, Hadith, Tafsir, and Arabic Grammar, dedicated to mentoring the next generation of scholars.

Our Values & Impact

Our values are firmly rooted in the doctrine set by Imam Ahmed Rida Khan and the pious predecessors (Salaf-e-Saaliheen). We take pride in our graduates, who are now serving as leading young scholars and Imams in Masjids across the North West.
